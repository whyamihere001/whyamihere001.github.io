2022-2023 Life-wide Learning Day

The “2022-2023 Life-wide Learning Day” was held at Hong Kong Disneyland on 18 November 2022. The Day offered experiential learning programme for our students to understand Disney’s corporate culture and work environment. Through the programme, students would be able to appreciate the importance of a positive work attitude and life skills, which facilitated their future career development.

Thanks giving service

The “True Light Girls' College 50th Anniversary Thanksgiving Service cum True Light Minihall and Future Classroom Opening Ceremony” was held on 5th May 2023. The service commenced with the sermon from Rev. Wong Ka Fai. It was followed by the unveiling of Time Capsule, which stored up many blessing items from our teachers and students in the 40th Anniversary Thanksgiving Service in 2013.
